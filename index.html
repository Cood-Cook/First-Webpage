The Legacy of Dragon Ball

From its humble beginnings as a manga in 1984 to its worldwide success as an anime, Dragon Ball has captured the hearts of millions. The story of Goku, the Saiyan warrior, continues to inspire new generations.

The Saiyan Bloodline

The Saiyans are a proud warrior race known for their incredible strength and ability to grow stronger after each battle. Goku, Vegeta, and their descendants carry this legacy forward, constantly breaking limits.

Legendary Battles

Who can forget Goku vs. Frieza on Namek, Gohan's Super Saiyan 2 awakening, or Jiren vs. Ultra Instinct Goku? These fights define Dragon Ball and showcase its legendary combat storytelling.
