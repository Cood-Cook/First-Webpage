Dragon Ball Facts

Here are some incredible facts about the Dragon Ball universe!

Goku's Original Name

Did you know? Goku's Saiyan birth name is Kakarot! It follows the Saiyan tradition of names based on vegetables.

Vegeta Was Supposed to be a Minor Character

Vegeta was initially planned as a short-term villain, but his popularity made him a mainstay in the series.

Toriyama Forgot Super Saiyan 2

Akira Toriyama admitted he sometimes forgets details of his own story, including the existence of Super Saiyan 2!
